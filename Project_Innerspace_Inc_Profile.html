Project Innerspace Profile

This is Project Innerspace

PI launched in 2022 with a two part plan. Step one, create a map of geothermal resources by 2024. Step two, roll out pilot programs in various locations for proof of concept.

The Highlights of Project Innerspace

Project Innerspace works to drill for thermal energy. What is thermal energy and how could it possibly be enviromentally friendly? Thermal energy is heat stored deep in the earth's crust. When it comes to drilling for hydrocarbons, when the oil pocket is emptied, it creates a void that could lead to sink holes. Alongside that, oil spills happen as well poluting the surroundings. Thermal energy on the other hand is just heat stored in rocks and there is no risk to poluting the environment. Project innerspace is activlly trying to make drilling for thermal energy economically feasible.
